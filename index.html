Welcome to Vardrum

Where the Black Wings Gather

The Saga of Black Wings is a dark epic fantasy series about wounded people who refuse to remain owned, named, silenced or diminished by the powers hunting them.

It is a world of ravens, wolves, hidden houses, salt roads, ancient bargains, animal witnesses, dangerous mercy and chosen family forged beneath pressure.

Here, love is not decoration. It is shelter, oath, witness, rebellion and sometimes war.

“A name remembered is a door the dark cannot fully close.”

The Heart of Black Wings

The Braid

The Braid is not a crown, bloodline or institution. It is a living bond between people who choose one another freely and continue choosing one another when the road turns cruel.

It holds warriors, survivors, lovers, children, families, neighbours, beasts, birds, spirits and remembered names.

Living Law

The Law of the Road

These truths run beneath the saga, older and stronger than the laws written by crowns.

Care freely given is not ransom. Names belong to the named. Origin is not ownership. Desperation is not permission. Bloodline is witness, not claim. A child is never proof. Refusal is a living law. The Braid is offer, not net.

Behind the Wings

Timothy M. Halliday

Timothy M. Halliday, writing as T.M.H, has carried The Saga of Black Wings for around forty years.

The saga grew from a lifetime of imagination, animals, friendship, grief, humour, neighbours, houses, roads, ravens, wolves, darkness and stubborn hope.

What began as a private world has become an expansive fantasy saga built to remember the names and lives that power would rather count as small.
