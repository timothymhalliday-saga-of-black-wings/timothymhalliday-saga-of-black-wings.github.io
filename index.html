The Saga

Saga of Black Wings is the vast dark fantasy world of Timothy M. Halliday: a mythic road of ravens, salt-marshes, old crowns, buried roots, beast-witness, family wounds, impossible love and hard-won refusal.

At its heart stand FATAS and Ness, the Raven and the Crow, walking through war, memory, danger, old magic and the terrible machinery of powers that try to own the living.

The saga is not only about battle. It is about what survives battle: children protected from prophecy, animals who know the truth first, families chosen in defiance of blood-law, and love strong enough to stand beside a broken world without becoming less human.

“We do not crown ourselves. We witness. We defend. We refuse.”

The Books

The saga currently runs through thirteen finished books, with Book XIV in development. Each book carries the road forward: from ash and pursuit to roots, crowns, agreement, refusal, the House that raised the Raven, and the House beyond the House.

Book I
Saga of Black Wings

The first road of ash, hunger, pursuit and survival. The Braid begins before anyone has a clean name for it.

Book II
The Reckoner’s Knot

Old debts tighten, choices knot, and the road demands a price from every survivor.

Book III
The Crown of Thorns and Salt

Crown-law, salt-marsh and old claims rise together, sharpening the war against ownership.

Book IV
The Crown Unmade

Authority cracks, but unmaking a crown does not unmake everything it taught the world.

Book V
The War of the Underroot

Beneath the broken Crown, older hunger wakes and reaches through root, blood and memory.

Book VI
The Rising Root

The buried war deepens as the Braid faces corruption patient enough to look natural.

Book VII
The Severing War

Severance becomes sacred, dangerous and necessary. To cut cleanly, the Braid must first know what must never be cut away.

Book VIII
The Last Root

The root-war reaches toward its final hunger: completion, control and the erasure of choice.

Book IX
The War Without Root

After the root breaks, the world does not become safe. It becomes unfinished.

Book X
The Quieting World

Quiet becomes a weapon, peace becomes suspicious, and silence learns to behave like power.

Book XI
The Agreement of Ash

False mercy rises in ash-coloured clothing, offering relief with hooks inside it.

Book XII
The Last Refusal

Refusal becomes sacred ground. Care must not become ownership. Protection must still ask.

Book XIII
The House That Raised the Raven

FATAS faces the House that shaped him, and Ness stands beside him where old rooms still breathe.

Book XIV
The House Beyond the House

In development. The next road turns north toward hidden family, Skeld-Fell, cold hearths, beast-law and ransom.

About the Author

Timothy M. Halliday is the creator of the Saga of Black Wings, a sprawling dark fantasy epic of ravens, roots, crowns, salt-marshes, beasts, old magic, family wounds and love that refuses to be owned.

His world is built from mythic violence, fierce tenderness, strange humour, animal loyalty and the belief that even in the darkest road, chosen family can become a form of resistance.
