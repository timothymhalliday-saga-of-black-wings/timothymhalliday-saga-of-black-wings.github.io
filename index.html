The Map of Everything

Welcome to the public map-room of The Saga of Black Wings, a dark epic fantasy world of ravens, wolves, salt-marsh roads, old law, chosen family, dangerous mercy, hidden houses, odes, songs, beasts, roots, crowns, refusal and love that will not be owned.

This page is the front gate. From here, readers can find the books, the Braid, the rogues, the lore, the odes, the maps, the visual archive and the author behind the long road.

“A map is not only where things are. Sometimes it is what survived being lost.”

The Law of the Road

These are some of the living laws that run beneath the saga.

Care freely given is not ransom. Names belong to the named. Origin is not ownership. Desperation is not permission. Bloodline is witness, not claim. A child is never proof. Refusal is a living law. The Braid is offer, not net.

The Author

Timothy M. Halliday, writing as T.M.H, has carried and shaped The Saga of Black Wings for around forty years.

The saga is deeply personal: a lifetime of imagination, animals, grief, humour, wounds, friendship, neighbours, roads, houses, ravens, wolves, love, darkness and stubborn hope transformed into epic fantasy.
