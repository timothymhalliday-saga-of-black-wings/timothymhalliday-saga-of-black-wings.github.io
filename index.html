About the Saga

Saga of Black Wings is a dark epic fantasy series set among salt-marshes, haunted roads, ancient powers, ravens, dogs, oaths, wounds, and stubborn hope.

It follows FATAS, Ness, the Braid, and their companions through wars of bloodline, crown, root, ash, refusal, family, and the living law of witness.
