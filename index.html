Welcome to the Black Wings Saga

The Saga of Black Wings follows FATAS, Ness and the Braid through a world where power wears many masks: crowns, roots, laws, ash, houses, ledgers and smiling forms of mercy that arrive with chains hidden beneath the cloth.

Across the saga, children, beasts, spirits, warriors and wounded families stand against systems that try to count them, own them, erase them or make their pain useful.

“Care freely given. Never ransom. Never proof.”

The World

Vardrum is a realm of salt-marsh roads, old druid law, broken halls, beast-witness, hidden rivers, watchful birds and dangerous houses. Its enemies are not always monsters. Sometimes they are systems. Sometimes they are traditions. Sometimes they are the old answer everyone stopped questioning.

The Road So Far

The Saga of Black Wings is a dark epic fantasy sequence following FATAS, Ness and the Braid through wars of crown, root, ash, law, family and witness.

Books I to XIII are complete. Book XIV is in development.

Finished Books

Book I

Saga of Black Wings

The first road opens: ravens, wolves, ash, danger and the beginning of the Braid’s refusal.

Book II

The Reckoner’s Knot

Knots of law, debt, precedent and violence tighten around the living.

Book III

The Crown of Thorns and Salt

The Crown’s reach widens, old salt-roads stir and deeper laws begin to bite.

Book IV

The Crown Unmade

Old birds, old signs, Braid-Hand and crown-breaking truths move into the light.

Book V

The War of the Underroot

The buried world wakes. Roots, bloodlines, old commands and ancient hunger reach upward.

Book VI

The Rising Root

The Underroot grows bolder, and the Braid learns that some wars begin beneath the floor.

Book VII

The Severing War

Severing becomes both weapon and mercy as the war against root-command grows savage.

Book VIII

The Last Root

The final root-pressure gathers, testing witness, family, refusal and every law carried.

Book IX

The War Without Root

A new wrongness rises where the world itself seems to ask what deserves to remain.

Book X

The Quieting World

Silence, absence and hidden judgment press against the living.

Book XI

The Agreement of Ash

Ash offers mercy with terms beneath it. Peace can become another kind of prison.

Book XII

The Last Refusal

Children, names, breath, care and witness stand at the edge of final settling.

Book XIII

The House That Raised the Raven

FATAS and Ness go home, where childhood, family wounds and old rooms become the battlefield.

In Development

Book XIV

The House Beyond the House

Book XIV has not started yet. The road ahead turns toward Elland’s hidden family, Skeld-Fell, cold hearths, beast-law, ransom, snow, old northern secrets and the question of what bloodline can witness without owning.

Core law: desperation is not permission.
