Welcome to the Black Wings

Saga of Black Wings is the dark fantasy world of Timothy M. Halliday: a road of salt-marshes, ravens, old crowns, buried roots, beasts, family wounds, oath-law, sacred refusal and love that refuses to be owned.

At its heart stand FATAS and Ness, the Raven and the Crow, walking through war, memory, grief, humour, old magic and powers that try to make people smaller than they are.

“We do not crown ourselves. We witness. We defend. We refuse.”

The Saga Shelf

Thirteen finished books carry the road from ash and pursuit to roots, crowns, agreement, refusal and the House that raised the Raven. Book XIV is in development.

About the Author

Timothy M. Halliday is the creator of Saga of Black Wings, a sprawling dark fantasy epic of ravens, crows, roots, crowns, salt-marshes, beasts, old magic, family wounds and love that refuses to be owned.

His world is built from mythic violence, fierce tenderness, strange humour, animal loyalty and the belief that chosen family can become a form of resistance.
