The Saga

Saga of Black Wings is a dark epic fantasy series by Timothy M. Halliday, set among salt-marshes, haunted roads, living runes, ancient houses, broken crowns, deep roots, oath-bound beasts, and the stubborn fire of chosen family.

At its heart are FATAS, Ness, the Braid, the ravens, the dogs, the wolves, the children who refuse to become proof, and the old powers that learn too late that love is not a leash.

This is a world of witness and refusal, where roads remember, houses listen, names matter, animals judge, and no crown has the final word.

Timothy M. Halliday

Timothy M. Halliday is the author of Saga of Black Wings, a mythic fantasy saga of ravens, salt-marshes, haunted roads, chosen family, resistance, old wounds, beasts who refuse collars, and love that will not be owned.

His work blends dark folklore, emotional survival, epic conflict, humour, family law, ancient power, and the stubborn human refusal to become what cruelty named us.
