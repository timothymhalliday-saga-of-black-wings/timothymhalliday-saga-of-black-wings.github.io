The Witness Archive

Canon Creature Standards of the Braid

They are not pets. They are not symbols. They are witnesses. Each carries memory. Each holds a role. Each serves the truth in their own way.

Every creature within The Saga of Black Wings carries witness in its own fashion. They are companions, protectors, guides, hunters, comforters and keepers of memory.

They are never ornaments and never decorations. They belong to the House as surely as any person who walks its roads.

This archive plate serves as the official visual reference for the creatures of the saga. Future illustrations, book artwork and commissioned paintings should follow these canonical standards so every appearance remains faithful throughout the world of Vardrum.

“We remember them. They remember us. Together, we keep the long road honest.”

The Braid Witness Banner

The Braid witness banner gathers the animal witnesses around the central oath: Witness, Refusal, Beside.

Mylo is the sandy tan Siamese-like cat. Shane is the red salt-hound. Bella burns at the centre as spirit-witness. No beast here is ornament. Every one carries memory.
