The Witness Archive

Canon Creature Standards of the Braid

"They are not pets. They are not symbols. They are witnesses. Each carries memory. Each holds a role. Each serves the truth in their own way."

Every creature within The Saga of Black Wings carries witness in its own fashion. They are companions, protectors, guides, hunters, comforters and keepers of memory. They are never ornaments and never decorations. They belong to the House as surely as any person who walks its roads.

This archive plate serves as the official visual reference for the creatures of the saga. Future illustrations, book artwork and commissioned paintings follow these canonical designs so that every appearance remains faithful throughout the world of Vardrum.

"We remember them. They remember us. Together, we keep the long road honest."
