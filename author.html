Forty Years Becoming One World

Some stories arrive in a season. Others spend decades gathering their own weather.

The Saga of Black Wings belongs to the second kind. For over forty years Timothy M. Halliday has built a world of kingdoms, marshes, forests, forgotten roads, ancient law, witness marks, animals, impossible friendships, quiet courage and people who refuse to be measured by crowns.

The series draws upon folklore, mythology, humour, grief, hope and the small moments that often matter more than battles. Every book adds another path through a living world whose history stretches far beyond the page.

"Forty years in the making.
One saga still unfolding."

The Heart of the Saga

Black Wings is epic fantasy, but it is equally a story about loyalty, family, endurance, laughter, betrayal, kindness and the remarkable bond between people and animals.

Readers will find ravens that remember, wolves that judge, forests with their own laws, forgotten kings, impossible romances, songs, odes, ancient customs and ordinary people discovering extraordinary courage.

The world may be dark, but it is never without light. Hope is earned, friendship matters, and love often proves stronger than power.

Looking Forward

The Saga of Black Wings continues to grow, with many books already written and later volumes expanding the mythology, history and living world still further.

This website exists as both a companion to the novels and a place where readers can explore maps, characters, lore, artwork, odes and the wider history surrounding the saga.
