# Saga of Black Wings

Official website for **The Saga of Black Wings** by **Timothy M. Halliday**, writing as **T.M.H**.

## About the Saga

*The Saga of Black Wings* is an epic fantasy world more than forty years in the making.

It is a long-form fantasy saga of ravens, roads, crowns, roots, houses, hidden families, old magic, loyal animals, songs, odes, humour, grief, betrayal, love, refusal, and the stubborn hope of people and creatures who refuse to be owned by the powers that shaped them.

## Author

**Timothy M. Halliday** has carried and shaped *The Saga of Black Wings* for around forty years. The saga is a deeply personal and imaginative work, built from decades of thought, emotion, myth, animals, memory, humour, darkness, warmth, and story.
