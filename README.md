## About the Saga

*The Saga of Black Wings* is a dark epic fantasy world carried and developed for around forty years.

The saga follows ravens, roads, crowns, roots, houses, hidden families, chosen bonds, loyal animals, old songs, humour, grief, betrayal, love, refusal and the stubborn hope of people and creatures who refuse to be owned by the powers that shaped them.

Fifteen books already stand within the manuscript. The later road through **Books XVI to XX** is now being developed.

## Safe Editing Rules

1. Edit **one file at a time**.
2. Copy the complete replacement file rather than pasting partial code.
3. Do not rename image files already used by the website.
4. Do not change `CNAME` unless the official domain changes.
5. Do not add `404.html` to the sitemap.
6. Keep only one sitemap named exactly `sitemap.xml`.
7. Test navigation after changing a page.
8. Keep public descriptions spoiler-safe.
9. Written canon outranks an older image label.
10. Keep the motto written exactly as:

   **No crown shall count us smaller.**

## Important Canon Guardrails

- **Brokan** is the correct spelling.
- **Lily** uses one `l`.
- **Myah** is the spoiler-sensitive antagonist name. Do not replace it with Maya.
- Scarlet is a **female crow**.
- Blue is a **male raven**.
- Scarlet and Blue are bonded animal witnesses, not human romance.
- Sarah and Craig are the parents of Joey and Lily.
- Josie is the mother of Katy and Jenny.
- Book XVI is **The Braid: Part II**.
- Book XVII is **Green Deer-Wood**.
- Public copy must not expose the exact Myah betrayal.
- Animal witnesses must be presented as living participants, never decoration.
