The Long Road

The Saga So Far

The Saga of Black Wings is a long-form dark epic fantasy series shaped by ravens, wolves, salt-marsh roads, old houses, buried law, chosen family and love that refuses ownership.

Fifteen books already exist within the manuscript. Work has also begun on Books XVI to XX, carrying the saga into new lands, deeper bonds and older dangers.

The summaries below remain deliberately spoiler-safe. The locked doors stay locked until readers reach them.

Books I to IV

The First Reckonings

Book I

Saga of Black Wings

The first road opens beneath black wings. Wounded lives begin to gather, old powers take notice and a family starts forming where no crown intended one.

Book II

The Reckoner’s Knot

Debt, law and witness tighten around the living. The cost of being counted by hostile hands becomes impossible to ignore.

Book III

The Crown of Thorns and Salt

Crown pressure deepens across road, house and family. Power learns to hide inside procedure, silence and fear.

Book IV

The Crown Unmade

Old truths begin breaking through the foundations of power as living refusal answers the Crown.

Books V to VIII

The Root Wars

Book V

The War of the Underroot

Buried forces rise beneath the visible world. Root-law hungers, old systems awaken and the Braid enters a war fought below ordinary sight.

Book VI

The Rising Root

The root-war grows stranger, sharper and more intimate as ancient powers reach again for living names.

Book VII

The Severing War

Bonds, loyalties and claims are tested where severance becomes weapon, threat and battlefield.

Book VIII

The Last Root

The oldest root-logic faces what it cannot simplify: living choice, dangerous love and refusal freely given.

Books IX to XII

The World After Root

Book IX

The World Without Root

After root-power breaks, the world must discover what remains when an ancient system no longer holds it together.

Book X

The Quieting World

Quiet becomes pressure, peace becomes danger and silence begins demanding too much from the living.

Book XI

The Agreement of Ash

Agreement, ash, witness and false mercy gather into a new threat wearing a calmer face.

Book XII

The Last Refusal

One of the saga’s deepest laws comes fully alive: refusal is not emptiness. It is a living door.

Books XIII to XV

House, Road and Braid

Book XIII

The House That Raised the Raven

Home, family, childhood and origin are brought into the fire and made to answer for what they remember.

Book XIV

The House Beyond the House

Hidden kin, old family law and unfamiliar roads draw the saga beyond the house it thought it understood.

Book XV

The Braid

The living bonds of the saga gather beneath one name. Love, family, animals, witness and refusal become stronger together without becoming a chain.

“No crown shall count us smaller.”

Books XVI to XX

The Roads Now Opening

Work has begun on Books XVI to XX.

These books continue the story of the Braid, its loves, its animals, its old enemies and the living laws carried from one generation of witness into the next.

New roads are opening through forest, house, memory, kinship and powers older than the maps that tried to contain them.

Further titles and story details will be revealed when their proper hour arrives.

“The map ends only where courage stops drawing.”
