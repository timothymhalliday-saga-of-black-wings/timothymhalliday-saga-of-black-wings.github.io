The Road So Far

The Saga of Black Wings follows FATAS, Ness, the Braid, the animals, the children, the spirits and the stubborn living through a dark fantasy war against crowns, roots, agreement, false mercy, old houses and powers that try to make people smaller than they are.

The entries below are spoiler-safe. They give the flavour of each book without opening the locked rooms too early.

Finished Books

Book I
Saga of Black Wings

The first road begins in ash, hunger and pursuit. FATAS, Ness and the early Braid are forced onto a road where survival, children, beasts and old enemies begin to bind together into something no crown can easily break.

Finished First Road FATAS & Ness

Book II
The Reckoner’s Knot

Old choices tighten. Debts, oaths and wounds begin to knot around the Braid, forcing every survivor to reckon with what the road has already taken and what it still demands.

Finished Reckoning Oaths

Book III
The Crown of Thorns and Salt

Crown-law, thorn, salt and old claims rise together. The world widens, the danger sharpens, and the Braid begins to understand how deeply power believes it can own the living.

Finished Crown Law Salt-Marsh

Book IV
The Crown Unmade

Authority begins to crack. The Crown’s old shape is challenged, broken and unmade, but the Braid learns that destroying a crown does not destroy everything it taught the world to fear.

Finished The Crown Unmaking

Book V
The War of the Underroot

Beneath broken authority, older hunger wakes. The Underroot reaches through soil, blood, memory and buried law, forcing the Braid to fight what lives below foundations.

Finished Underroot Buried War

Book VI
The Rising Root

The root-war deepens. Corruption becomes patient, old magic sharpens, and the Braid faces an enemy that does not merely attack the world, but tries to become part of it.

Finished Root-Mother Old Magic

Book VII
The Severing War

Severance becomes sacred and dangerous. War, romance, children, signs and beasts collide as the Braid learns that to cut cleanly, one must first know what must never be cut away.

Finished Severance Romance

Book VIII
The Last Root

The root-war reaches toward its final hunger. The Braid confronts a power that wants completion, control and the erasure of choice dressed as peace.

Finished Last Root Choice

Book IX
The War Without Root

After the root breaks, the world does not become safe. It becomes unfinished. Absence, memory, agreement and identity begin to shift, and reality itself starts asking what is worth keeping.

Finished Absence Agreement

Book X
The Quieting World

Quiet becomes a weapon. Peace becomes suspicious. The Braid faces a world learning softer ways to erase, where silence can become more dangerous than a shout.

Finished The Quiet False Peace

Book XI
The Agreement of Ash

False mercy rises in ash-coloured clothing. The Agreement offers relief, shelter and order, but every soft promise carries hooks beneath its skin.

Finished Ash Law False Mercy

Book XII
The Last Refusal

Refusal becomes sacred ground. The saga turns toward consent, care, distance, danger and the hard truth that protection must never become ownership.

Finished Refusal Consent

Book XIII
The House That Raised the Raven

The war turns inward. FATAS faces the House that shaped him, Ness stands beside him, and the saga asks whether a person can be shaped by violence without belonging to it.

Finished The House Chosen Home
