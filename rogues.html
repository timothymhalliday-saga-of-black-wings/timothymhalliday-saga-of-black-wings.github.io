Before the Dark Names Begin

Harm Has Many Faces

The enemies of The Saga of Black Wings are not only people carrying blades.

Some arrive as offices. Some as roots. Some as kindness with hooks beneath it. Some as laws that smile while quietly closing the door.

This gallery remains spoiler-safe. It names the major threat-shapes of the saga without opening the deepest sealed rooms too early.

The Laws Black Wings Fights

Enemy Laws

Need makes permission. Care creates debt. Blood creates ownership. Safety cancels refusal. Children can become proof. Animals must be controlled. Silence means consent. Origin decides destiny. A softer voice makes a chain kinder. A signed paper can replace a living no.
“Ask what the kindness costs. Ask who holds the road. Ask whether the living may still say no.”
