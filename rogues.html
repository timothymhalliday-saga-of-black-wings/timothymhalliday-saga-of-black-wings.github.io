Before the Dark Names Begin

Harm Has Many Faces

The enemies of The Saga of Black Wings are not only people carrying blades. Some arrive as offices. Some as roots. Some as kindness with hooks beneath it. Some as laws that smile while quietly closing the door.

This gallery names the major shapes of danger without opening the saga’s deepest sealed rooms. It records not only villains, but the systems, beliefs and hungers that teach ordinary people to participate in harm.

Spoiler seal: descriptions reveal the flavour of each threat, not its final fate, hidden alliances or deepest connection to the Braid.
