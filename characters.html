Before the Names Begin

The Living Heart of the Saga

The Saga of Black Wings is built from people who survive, choose, remember and refuse.

Some arrive carrying blades. Some arrive carrying children, grief, secrets, medicine, food, feathers, scars or old songs. Some never speak with human tongues at all.

This page is spoiler-safe. It offers names, bonds and flavour without opening the saga’s locked doors too early.

The Living Braid

The Central Bonds

The Braid is not an institution, bloodline or command. It is a freely chosen bond between lives that continue to stand beside one another when standing becomes costly.

Raven-Hearted Warrior

FATAS

Oath-bearer, survivor, wound-carrier and storm-centre of the saga. His greatest strength is not conquest, but the refusal to stop rising.

Beloved and Fellow Traveller

Ness

Fierce, loving and clear-eyed. Ness does not arrive as reward or rescue, but as a woman willing to walk beside a wounded warrior without owning his road.

Crown-Threaded Survivor

Cael

Clever, dangerous and difficult to read. Cael survives among hidden pressures while trying to remain loyal to truths stronger than command.

Scarred Protector

Brokan

Powerful, guarded and impossible to reduce to a simple judgement. Beneath the scars stands a man learning what freely chosen loyalty can become.

Witness of Courage

Lucy

A presence of love, memory and stubborn courage. Lucy carries warmth without softness becoming weakness.

Road-Loyal Companion

Jesse

Brave, grounded and loyal through difficult roads. Jesse stands where friendship becomes action rather than promise.

Keeper of Signs

Eldwyn

Hearth-bound guide, reader of old knowledge and keeper of practical truths. Eldwyn brings wisdom with mud still on its boots.

Strong-Hearted Witness

Ulrike

Loyal, sharp-minded and difficult to shake. Ulrike brings grit, honesty and strength to the places where others might look away.

Quiet Mystery

James

Wounded, thoughtful and road-marked. James carries hidden weight and one of the saga’s most quietly important hearts.

Fierce Heart of the Future

Maisie

Daughter, witness and living thread of family, refusal and future law. Maisie is never merely someone to be protected.

Family Is a Living Verb

Hearth and Familia

Mother and Hearth-Witness

Sarah

A mother whose care is practical, fierce and grounded in the daily work of keeping a family alive.

Father and Steady Hand

Craig

A family man of quiet strength, warmth and ordinary courage. Craig stands as proof that gentleness can hold its ground.

Daughter of the Hearth

Lily

Bright, observant and woven into the future of the living Braid. Her name is carried with one “l”.

Son of the Hearth

Joey

Child of Sarah and Craig, carrying curiosity, courage and the future-facing law of the family.

Mother of Three

Josie

Mother of Katy and Jenny, tied to love, care, endurance and the grounded strength of family.

Daughter and Future-Thread

Katy

A young life of courage, tenderness and future promise, whose presence changes the shape of what comes next.

Daughter and Hearth-Light

Jenny

Warm, human and deeply woven into the ordinary love that keeps great stories from becoming hollow.

Grounded Companion

Taff

A thread of humour, loyalty and practical warmth within the wider Braid-world.

Fire, Spirit and Memory

Kian and Bella

Bell-Light and Spirit Warrior

Kian

A brave remembered name whose courage continues to echo through the saga. Small in years, never small in meaning.

Spirit Guide

Bella

A loving presence of gentleness, memory and guidance. Bella’s light is warm, but never defenceless.

Living Spirit Flame

Bella’s Flame

A sacred flame of courage, memory and refusal. It burns beside the Braid without becoming anyone’s possession.

Scent, Road and Hidden Truth

Those Who Read What Others Conceal

Scent-Bearer and Tracker

Jórunn

Sharp, instinctive and difficult to deceive. Jórunn reads scent, old-road guilt and truths people hoped their bodies would hide.

Watcher of Hidden Roads

Elland

Keeper of signs, secrets and old warnings. Elland knows too much, says too little and carries a history Jórunn may be able to smell.

The Scent Woman

Vanessa

A woman tied to scent, memory and recognition, carrying a presence that reaches beyond what eyes alone can understand.

Druid Forest-Keeper

Kelda Veyrún

Beautiful, powerful and alive with old forest law. Kelda listens, prunes, heals, protects and knows when rot must finally be cut away.

The Wounded Road

Lives Under Pressure

Not every important figure arrives cleanly. Some come frightened, guilty, divided, manipulated, hunted or carrying harm they do not yet understand.

Daughter of Divided Roads

Mia

A survivor carrying grief, fire and the difficult work of becoming herself beneath dangerous pressure.

The Tangled Trust

Myah

A deeply complicated figure tied to Mia, love, family-law and one of the Braid’s most painful fractures. Her full road remains behind a locked door.

Wounded Traveller

Bran

A troubled figure carrying debt, pain and the difficult possibility that change may still be chosen.

Sharp Survivor

Phoebe “Phebs”

Wounded, clever and caught between guilt, survival, danger and difficult mercy.

Road-Shadow

Riven

Marked by old hurt and the kind of survival that leaves teeth behind.

Protected Name

Lark

A vulnerable, carried name bound to tenderness, danger and the law that the vulnerable must not be abandoned.

Beast and Bird Witnesses

Those Who Speak Without Human Tongues

The animals of Black Wings are not decoration. They warn, judge, protect, remember, expose lies and sometimes understand the law before the people do.

Female Crow Witness

Scarlet

Calm, sturdy and sharply observant. Scarlet carries restrained garnet in her feathers and judgement in her gaze.

Male Raven Witness

Blue

Slimmer, inquisitive and alive with cobalt-indigo iridescence. Blue is messenger, objector and professional complainer.

Dog Witness

Mad

Loyal, alert and full of practical animal sense. Mad guards hearth, road and family.

Brindle Dog Witness

Benson

A steady companion and living argument against treating animals as tools or property.

Black Dog Witness

Hugo

Black-coated with white paws and ear tips. Hugo carries loyalty, instinct and watchfulness.

Russet Dog Witness

Shane

A red-russet dog bound to pain, fear, loyalty and the complicated memory of survival.

Sandy Cat Witness

Mylo

A sandy, Siamese-like cat, impossible courier, object-guardian and smug vanisher of great importance.

Moor-Wolf

Jack

A shifting-coloured wolf of old animal law, hidden leashes and wild judgement.

Wing-Shadow Bird

Dusk

Quiet, watchful and dark-winged. Dusk reads old routes from the edges of sight.

How to Read the Names

Fragments, Not Verdicts

These entries are introductions, not final judgements. Some people change. Some fall. Some return. Some are loved before they are understood.

“The Braid does not ask whether a name is tidy. It asks whether the living are still there.”
